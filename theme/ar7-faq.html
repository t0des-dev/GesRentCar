Reservation

How do I reserve a vehicle?

Choose a vehicle from our fleet, select your dates and location, and confirm your reservation online. Instant vehicles are confirmed immediately after your 30% deposit; partner vehicles are confirmed once availability is verified.

Can I modify my reservation?

Yes, contact our team by phone or WhatsApp and we'll update your dates, location or vehicle whenever possible.

Can I cancel my reservation?

Yes, free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before pickup, according to our cancellation policy.

How long does reservation confirmation take?

AR7 fleet vehicles confirm instantly after deposit. Partner vehicles are typically confirmed within a few hours.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfer for select reservations.

Why do I only pay a 30% deposit?

The deposit secures your reservation while keeping the process flexible. The remaining balance is paid at vehicle pickup.

When do I pay the remaining balance?

The remaining 70% is payable directly at pickup, by card or cash.

Can I receive an invoice?

Yes, invoices are available on request, particularly for corporate and long-term rentals.

Is online payment secure?

Yes, all payments are encrypted and processed securely. We never store your full card details.

Partner Vehicles

AR7 collaborates with carefully selected rental partners across Morocco, and every partner follows AR7 quality standards. For these vehicles, availability is verified first; once confirmed, you receive a confirmation and are invited to pay the 30% deposit — this guarantees transparency and avoids double bookings.
How does the partner fleet work?

AR7 works with trusted local partners to offer a larger fleet while maintaining the same inspection and quality standards as our own vehicles.

Do I pay before or after confirmation?

Always after. No payment is requested until the partner has confirmed the vehicle is available.

Is the experience different from AR7's own fleet?

No — every partner vehicle is inspected and held to the same standard, so your experience is identical.

Airport Delivery

Can you deliver to Marrakech Airport?

Yes, airport delivery is available at Marrakech Menara, along with Casablanca, Tangier and Rabat.

Can you monitor my flight?

Yes, we monitor your flight to ensure timely vehicle delivery, even in case of delays.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

We automatically adjust your delivery time based on your flight's real arrival, at no extra cost.

Is airport delivery available 24/7?

Yes, our airport counters are open 24 hours a day, every day.

Insurance

Is insurance included?

Yes, every rental includes comprehensive insurance and 24/7 roadside assistance at no extra cost.

Can I purchase additional insurance?

Yes, Full Insurance with zero deductible can be added as a rental extra during checkout.

What happens in case of an accident?

Contact our 24/7 emergency line immediately; our team will guide you through the process and arrange a replacement vehicle if needed.

Vehicle Pickup & Return

Where do I collect my vehicle?

At the airport counter or city office you selected during booking; the exact address is confirmed in your reservation.

Can I return the vehicle in another city?

Yes, one-way rentals between our Casablanca, Marrakech, Rabat, and Tangier locations are available on request.

What happens if I return the vehicle late?

A grace period of 29 minutes is allowed; late returns are billed per hour after that.

Rental Conditions

What is the minimum age to rent?

Drivers must be at least 21 years old for most categories, or 23+ for luxury vehicles.

What are the driving license requirements?

A valid driver's license held for at least one year (two years for luxury vehicles) is required.

Are foreign licenses accepted?

Yes, most foreign licenses are accepted; an International Driving Permit is recommended but not always required.

What is the mileage policy?

Every rental includes unlimited mileage, with no hidden per-kilometer fees.

What is the fuel policy?

Vehicles are provided full and should be returned full — like-for-like.

What is the deposit policy?

A refundable security deposit is held on your card at pickup, in addition to the 30% reservation deposit.

Corporate Rental

Can companies rent multiple vehicles?

Yes, our Corporate Mobility service supports multi-vehicle contracts with a single invoice.

Do you offer monthly rental?

Yes, Long-Term Rental plans are available for a month or more, with better pricing and fleet upgrade options.

Do you provide business invoicing?

Yes, consolidated business invoicing is available for corporate accounts.

Do I get a dedicated account manager?

Yes, every corporate account is assigned a dedicated account manager.

Luxury Collection

Can I reserve luxury vehicles?

Yes, the full Luxury Collection can be reserved online or through our concierge team.

Is concierge service available?

Yes, our concierge team can help you choose the right luxury vehicle for your trip via WhatsApp or phone.

Do luxury vehicles require additional verification?

Some luxury vehicles may require a higher security deposit and additional ID verification at pickup.

General Questions

Do you operate across Morocco?

Yes, AR7 Victoria Car and its partner network cover 15+ cities across Morocco.

How quickly do you reply?

We typically respond within a few hours during office hours; WhatsApp is usually fastest.

Can I contact you through WhatsApp?

Yes, WhatsApp is our preferred and fastest contact method.

Do you speak English and French?

Yes, our team assists customers in English, French, and Arabic.
