FAQ

Common questions about our services.

Do you deliver to all airports?

Yes, airport delivery is available at Casablanca, Marrakech, Tangier and Rabat, with flight monitoring included.

Can companies rent multiple vehicles?

Yes — our Corporate Mobility service supports multi-vehicle contracts with a single invoice and dedicated account manager.

Can I rent for several months?

Yes, our Long-Term Rental plans are built for stays of a month or more, with better pricing and fleet upgrade options.

How does the partner fleet work?

Partner vehicles are inspected and held to the same AR7 standards, so your experience is identical regardless of ownership.
