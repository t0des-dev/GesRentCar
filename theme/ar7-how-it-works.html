Reservation Journey

Seven steps, zero uncertainty.

From choosing your vehicle to driving away, here's exactly what happens at every stage.

01

Choose Your Vehicle

Browse our fleet and select the vehicle that best fits your needs — Luxury, SUV, Economy or Business.

LuxurySUVEconomyBusiness
02

Select Your Rental Details

Choose your pickup location, pickup date & time, return location, and return date & time. If airport delivery is selected, you can provide your flight information so we can monitor it.

03

Reservation Request

After selecting your vehicle, submit your reservation request. At this stage, no commitment is required until availability has been verified, if necessary.

04

Availability Verification

This step depends on the type of vehicle you selected.

Instant Confirmation

For vehicles owned directly by AR7 Victoria Car, availability is confirmed instantly and you proceed directly to deposit payment.

Partner Vehicle

For vehicles managed by trusted partner agencies, our team verifies availability before requesting payment. You receive confirmation as quickly as possible.

This process prevents double bookings and guarantees a reliable reservation, every time.
05

Deposit Payment

Once your reservation is confirmed, you pay a 30% deposit. The remaining balance is paid upon vehicle pickup.

Secure online payment Bank transfer accepted More methods coming soon
06

Vehicle Preparation

Our team prepares the vehicle: professional cleaning, technical inspection, fuel check, and final quality control. If airport delivery is selected, the vehicle is delivered according to your arrival time.

07

Pickup & Enjoy Your Journey

Collect your vehicle, complete the final paperwork, pay the remaining balance, and enjoy your trip across Morocco.

FAQ

Common questions about the process.

Why do some vehicles require confirmation?

Vehicles managed by our trusted partner network need availability verified first, so we never risk a double booking.

Why do I only pay a 30% deposit?

The deposit secures your reservation while keeping the process flexible. The remaining balance is paid at vehicle pickup.

Can I modify my reservation?

Yes, contact our team by phone or WhatsApp and we'll update your dates, location or vehicle whenever possible.

How long does confirmation take?

AR7 fleet vehicles confirm instantly after deposit. Partner vehicles are typically confirmed within a few hours.
